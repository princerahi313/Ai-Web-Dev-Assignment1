Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you register for DevConf 2026.

When and where is DevConf 2026?

DevConf 2026 takes place September 10–12, 2026 at the Moscone Center, San Francisco, CA. All sessions, workshops, and keynotes will be held on-site with a hybrid live-stream option for remote attendees.

What does my ticket include?

All tickets include full access to keynotes and technical talks. Pro and Team tickets additionally include workshop access, speaker meet-and-greets, VIP dinner, and lifetime recording access. Check the pricing section for a full breakdown.

What is the refund policy?

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event (August 10, 2026). After that, tickets can be transferred to another attendee at no charge. No refunds will be issued within 7 days of the event.

Will sessions be available online?

Yes! All keynotes and main-stage talks will be live-streamed. Pro and Team ticket holders receive permanent access to recordings of every session, including workshops, uploaded within 48 hours of the talk.

Is there a student or startup discount?

Absolutely. We offer a 40% discount for verified students and a 25% discount for startups with fewer than 10 employees. Apply through our website with a valid student ID or company details during checkout.

How can my company become a sponsor?

We have sponsorship tiers ranging from Gold to Platinum with benefits like branded booths, logo placement, and recruitment access. Reach out to our partnerships team at sponsors@devconf2026.com to get the full prospectus.
